Walking away after some years

Fidelity, 1869, Briton Rivière

I’ve been a founding member at a local HEMA club for nearly four years. It has been a huge part of my life, and I’m leaving behind some great friends. But ultimately, I cannot stay at a fencing club that lacks fencing.

The focus has shifted entirely toward compliant drills. Sparring has been reduced to just thirty minutes a month, maybe less. For a fencer who values pressure and the truth of the duel, that isn’t enough to sustain the work.
